About

Letflix is one of the world's leading media streaming platform and entertainment service that serves users the most exciting and excellent choices of entertainment media in the form of movies and TV shows / series. With the current selection of more than a dozen entertainment media, user satisfaction and time spent well is rest assured. Our history goes back to three computer science students from the Bina Nusanatara University in Indonesia as they were tasked with creating a project as a part of their final evaluation on the Human-Computer Interaction course that they were taking.
